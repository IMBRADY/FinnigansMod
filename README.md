## Features

### Expanded Combat

- Muskets and Blunderbusses
- Spears, Halberds, Cleavers, Knives, and Longbows
- Unique weapons with completely custom abilities
- Dozens of balanced enchantments

---

### Living Villages

Villages become civilizations instead of trading halls. Recruit warriors, appoint builders, become a Village Chief.

Watch villages grow, defend themselves, and fight raids as organized settlements.

---

### Adventure Beyond the End

Defeating the Ender Dragon is only the beginning.

Unlock:

- New structures
- Powerful bosses
- Unique civilizations
- Rare artifacts
- Endgame quests
- Unique legendary weapons

The world continues evolving long after vanilla progression ends.
